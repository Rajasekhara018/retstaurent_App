PettinOut: The Ultimutt Pet Event For You and Your Dog 24th March 2022/by Nandini Poddar

SteppinOut by Dineout, in association with Heads Up For Tails, brings to you PettinOut—the best pet event in your city along with some great stuff for your furry family. While this will be an excellent hangout for pet parents such as yourself, pet lovers can also come here and have a good time. And it doesn’t just end there—there are tons of fun activities, food & beverages, an exclusive HUFT pop-up store with products across categories including new launches and a chance to meet other animal lovers!
